World Cup Timeline

Key Milestones

  • 1930 - First tournament in Uruguay.
  • 1954 - First tournament to be televised.
  • 1958 - Pelé wins his first World Cup at 17.
  • 1970 - First tournament broadcast in color.
  • 1994 - First World Cup in the USA.
  • 1998 - The tournament expands to 32 teams.
  • 2022 - Messi leads Argentina to glory in Qatar.
  • 2026 - First tournament to feature 48 teams.
